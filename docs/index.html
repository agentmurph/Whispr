How It Works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

Press a hotkey, speak naturally, and your words appear wherever you're typing. It's that simple.

1
⌨️

Press the hotkey

Hit ⌥ Space from any app. A floating overlay appears, ready to listen.

⌥ Space
2
🎙️

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace. Watch the live waveform respond to your voice in real-time.

3
✨

Text appears

Your words are transcribed locally and typed into whatever app has focus. Magic.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for speed, privacy, and simplicity. No bloat, no cloud dependencies, no accounts.

🔒

Local & Private

All transcription happens on-device. Your voice data never leaves your Mac. Period. No telemetry, no analytics, no cloud.

⚡

CoreML Accelerated

Blazing fast on Apple Silicon with automatic CoreML encoder acceleration. Transcribe faster than real-time on M1 and later.

💬

Smart Text Injection

Types directly into any focused app via simulated keystrokes. Works with every text field across macOS.

🛡️

Secure Field Detection

Automatically detects password fields and secure inputs, falling back to clipboard paste to keep things working smoothly.

🎯

Per-App Hotkeys

Configure custom keyboard shortcuts for different apps. Your workflow, your rules, your shortcuts.

📊

Waveform Visualization

Real-time audio feedback with a beautiful waveform display. Know exactly when Whispr is hearing you.
